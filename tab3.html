What is SetUp?

A microblogging/social media app that gives users information on PC, Setups, troubleshooting etc, by the help of an interactive app where users can use the advantage to interact with each other and seek guidance about setups as well as watching educational videos related to pc.

The app also allows users to search, ask, or receive real-time support or assistance from helpers in the community or the app itself.

Our History

SetUp was formed on March 4, 2022 by the group of students from STI College Las Pinas from the strand of IT-MAWD as their project. In the middle of the pandemic, the group had a motivation to make a program that will help people be more knowledgeable about PCs. Due to the fact we are limited to go outside. Our group is expecting that the application will be a helpful guide to those who want to join the PC master race and as the world has growing tech exploration and innovations.

VISION

Envisions a society that is knowledgeable about technology and being a organization that is trusted by people in providing services in their own comfort.

MISSION

Aims to be a helping hand to people that are having difficulties in using PC's for their daily activities by providing quality services to the community.
